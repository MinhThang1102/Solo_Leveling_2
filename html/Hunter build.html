Hướng dẫn build các hunter khác

Cha Hae-In

The 9th S-Rank hunter discovered in South Korea and the vice guild master of the Hunters Guild. She was the strongest hunter in South Korea before Sung Jiwoo made his debut. Due to her special ability to smell mana, she goes about life with a handkerchief covering her nose. She is fascinated to discover that Sung Jiwoo is the only hunter she's ever encountered with a pleasant scent.

Go Gunhee

The S-Rank chairman of the Korean Hunters Association, known as "The Sky Above the Skies." Despite his outstanding power and abilities, he rarely stands on the frontlines due to his age and medical conditions.


Thomas Andre

A National Level Hunter and the guild master of the Scavenger Guild. He has the power to inflict overwhelming violence. Perhaps the knowledge that few can touch him accounts for his carefree and eccentric nature. He likes things that make his pulse race, and takes interest in the power that Sung Jinwoo display

Amamiya Mirei

A sword-wielding teen who engages in battle by borrowing the power of the warrior Oni Kuroha's soul. Since she's working as a hunter in secret, she always appears on the scene wearing an Oni mask. Because her personality is so different when she wears the mask and when she doesn't, it often feels like she's two different people.


Han Se-Mi

An A-Rank healer who can save anyone's life as long as they're still breathing. She joined the Hunters Guild's B-Team to support her four younger siblings. Because she knows that she could die at any moment, she always carries a notebook with her will in her inner pocket
